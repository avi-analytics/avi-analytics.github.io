Union Budget 2017

Union Budget is a gold mine of data and information. Useful information is often buried deep in it’s cracks and crevices. I ferret out a few numbers and graphs to compare projected tax collection and expenditure growth rate patterns with last year’s actual figures. Total annual expenditure of central government is budgeted at INR 2.1 trillion which is a meagre 6.56% higher than last year.

Health, Energy, Transport, Social Welfare, and IT have received above-average allocations; while Agriculture and Allied sectors, Defense, and Education have received average allocations. Agriculture and Allied sectors and Development of North East are interesting items where allocation has been sharply reduced in relative terms. Tax collection growth is mainly driven by excise tax.
